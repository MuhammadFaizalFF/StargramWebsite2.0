About Us

Stargram is for Creators to build and maintain an active community of your Superfans.

And also, for people to build direct connection with their favourite content Creators.

On Stargram Creators can create public communities (group) similar to WhatsApp group/ Telegram group/ Discord servers with unlimited group members.

Also, Creators can create private communities (groups) exclusive for your superfans. Creators can set membership plans for the private community and only those who subscribe to the membership plan will get access to the private community.

Creators have the option to generate a solid revenue stream from the Stargram app and any revenue generated will be transferred directly to the Creators bank account and Stargram charges only a nominal fee.

Creators can signup an account free of cost and to create communities you need to get verified.

To get a verified creator account you can either request through app (go the home screen > more button > request for verification) or
please write to hello@stargram.in with relevant information.

Any queries, suggestions or feedback please write to hello@stargram.in
